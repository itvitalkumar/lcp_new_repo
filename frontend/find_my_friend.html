What is this page?
Here you will find posts from fellow students who are looking for lost friends, batchmates, or classmates. Each post includes details like college, batch year, and a personal message. You can like, comment, or share a post to help someone reconnect. If you are searching for someone, use the "Post a Friend Search" tab to create your own post. Together, we can rebuild lost connections.
